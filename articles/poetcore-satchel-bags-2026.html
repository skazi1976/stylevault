Pinterest has declared it: ‘the poet aesthetic’ searches are up 175% in 2026, and the Poetcore trend is reshaping how we think about bags. Inspired by romantic literary characters, Poetcore style favors structured leather satchels, vintage totes, and bags that look like they’ve been carried through bookshops in Paris and libraries in London. It’s the opposite of fast fashion — these are bags with character, soul, and timeless appeal.
